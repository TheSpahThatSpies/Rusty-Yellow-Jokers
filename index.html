About the band

Rusty Yellow Jokers. Rusty yellow jokers are an eclectic stew of different genres. The main ingredients consist primarily of rock, blues and jazz, spiced up with dance-friendly rhythms from the west and south, and flavored with folk music from both the north and the east. Rusty Yellow Jokers serve instrumental stories with energy, vitality, emotion, groove, humor, cold sweats and psychedelic fever ....
